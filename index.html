Nestled in the heart of Gulberg II, Fashion Pakistan Lounge is Lahore's most distinguished multi-designer retail experience. We bring together Pakistan's most celebrated names under one meticulously curated roof.

From exquisite formal wear to luminous bridal collections, every piece in our lounge is chosen with an unwavering commitment to craft, heritage, and contemporary elegance.
